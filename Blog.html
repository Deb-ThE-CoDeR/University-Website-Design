Our Certificate & online Program for 2021

Quantum University shall conscientiously allow students to express and hone their aesthetic sensibilities in sports, theatre, dance, music, photography, design, art, and social work through a host of clubs and committees. Quantum could possibly be the only private University in India which has programs titled 'Passion Programmes' (PROPS) dedicated to an integrated study of such areas of human knowledge.


Your student days should be among the best days of your life. Quantum University has a thriving campus life full of intellectual opportunities, exciting adventures, and lasting friendships. Whether you're seeking academic excellence, passionate indulgence in your favourite sport, music or theatre, a vibrant cafeteria for hang-outs, outbound trips to explore the spectacular world beyond the campus, opportunities for social interaction, or just a safe and comfortable place to live, you'll have a great time Quantum. Full of fun and quite unforgettable!


Quantum University believes that passion and profession are the two faces of the single coin. One nourishes the body, the other feeds the soul. At Quantum, students are asked to take up Passion Programs as per their choice. Quantum, encourages the students to pursue their passion while studying at Quantum and excel in the field. Students no longer have to give up on their passion to pursue their careers. Through this the students have the chance to grow under a guided and structured format and achieve excellence in the chosen passion program. At the end of the program students would be expected to showcase their work in front of a live audience.
